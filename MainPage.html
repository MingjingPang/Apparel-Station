About Apparel Station


Apparel Station is a lifestyle retailer dedicated to inspiring customers through a unique combination of product, creativity and cultural understanding. Founded in 2020 in a small space across the street from the San Jose State University, Apparel Station now operates online and provides worldwide shipping, offering experiential virtual environments and a well-curated mix of on-trend women’s and men’s clothes, from dresses, denim and graphics, to a collection of handpicked vintage clothing. Shop online at ApparelStation.com and get free shipping on US orders over $50. For special offers and more, download the AS app. Follow, share and shop @Apparel Station.
